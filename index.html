The Problem

Your Product Might Work… But Does It Feel Right?

Most companies rely on testing methods that miss what matters most to users:

  • Automated testing suites
  • Internal QA teams
  • Developers testing their own product
  • Engineers who know the product too well

The problem? Real users experience things very differently. They don't share your mental model — and that gap costs you.
